SoDir allows you to make a list (we call it a directory) of all your social media accounts, blogs, basically anything with a web address, and to make that list easy to find for anyone you want to find it.

After signing up for SoDir, you'll get a shareable link. We suggest putting this link in the 'bio' section of your public social media accounts, so it's easier for people to find your other public social media accounts.
